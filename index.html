Tim & Marina

Our first date in 2017 took place at The Bitter Bar in Boulder, where we bonded over our nerdy interests and That Pink Drink (a Bitter Bar classic). Tim left the state to go back to school the next day, though he swears it was a great first date. We chatted on the phone almost every day, usually about totally normal questions. We learned more about the important things in each other’s lives: our family, our friends, and our dreams for the future. The following eight years have gone by in a flash:

  • October 2017: We became FaceBook Official.
  • March 2018: We moved into an apartment together by the train tracks in Louisville, Colorado.
  • June 2019: Winri the Couch Princess & Gutter Girl stole our hearts and filled our house with dog hair forevermore.
  • October 2020: Calcifer the Little Mister & Trouble Bean moved in and ensured we would never leave food out again.
  • March 2021: We bought our home in Longmont Colorado and fell in love with the town.
  • December 2023: Tim proposed to Marina at The Bitter Bar, where it all began.
  • June 2025: We're getting married and you're invited!
